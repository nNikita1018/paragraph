RAZZI is a 2018 indian hindi movie directed by Meghna Gulzar and produced by Vineet Jain,Karan Johar, Hiroo yash johar and Apoorva Mehta under the banners of junglee pictures and Dharma Production.

it stars Alia Bhatt and features Vickey Kushal,Rajit Kapoor , Shishir Shrama, and Jaideep Ahlawat in supporting Roles. The film is an adeption of Harender Sikka's 2008 novel Calling Sehmat, a true account of an indian Research and Analysis Wing agent who, upon her father's request ,is married into a family of miltary officials in pakistan to relay information in india , prior to the indo-pakistani war of 1971.

principal photography of Razzi began in july 2017 in mumbai and was completed on 27 october 2017 it was shot across several location including patila , nabha.

RAZZI was Relesed on 11 may 2018 made on a budget of 35 crore , RAZZI went on to gross 196 crore worldwide.it won five Awards including best film, best director , best Actress for Alia Bhatt. worldwide.it won five Awards including best film, best director , best Actress for Alia Bhatt.
